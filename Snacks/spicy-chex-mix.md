# Spicy Chex Mix

## Ingredients

- 3 Cups Corn Chex
- 3 Cups Rice Chex
- 3 Cups Wheat Chex
- 1 Cup Pretzels
- 1 Cup cheese crackers()
- 1/4 Cup butter
- 1 T Worcestershire
- 1.25 teaspoon Seasoned Salt
- 2-3 tea red pepper sauce

## Directions:
Generally follow standard chex mix process
mix it all, bake it low stirring every 15 for an hour
